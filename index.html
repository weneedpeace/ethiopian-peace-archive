About This Project

The Ethiopian Peace Archive preserves and amplifies the voices of everyday Ethiopians and global supporters who believe in peace, unity, and coexistence. Over 1,000 voices were collected across all Ethiopian regions—Tigray, Amhara, Oromia, Somali, Afar, and beyond—alongside messages of solidarity from around the world.

This archive stands as a permanent, searchable counter-narrative to conflict, built on what people actually want: peace.
