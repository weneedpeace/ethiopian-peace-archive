Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

The Ethiopian Peace Archive is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of all contributors. We collect voices, not personal data.

What We Collect

Voice Submissions: When you submit a peace message, we collect your message, region/country, and language. We do NOT publish names, phone numbers, emails, or any identifying information.

What We NEVER Do

❌ Never publish names or contact details
❌ Never sell or share data
❌ Never track individual users
❌ Never share contributor identities

Your Rights

Request deletion of your submission anytime by contacting us.
